About Us

We are the Supreme Student Council of Golden Success College for the school year 2023-2024. Our goal is to help the college students, and to make a change for the betterment of our beloved school for we plan to make our school Keen, Unified, Students Of Golden Success College. We have plenty of events in store and changes to implement, so sit tight, do your duties extraordinarily well, and look forward to this years school year 2023-2024!

Programs Held

Program 1: SSC Christmas Party

A christmas party specifically aimed for our fun for the college students which was held in the afternoon at the rooftop (6th floor) @ 4:00pm - 6:00pm.

Program 2: College's Teacher's Day

We held a tribute to our teachers who worked tirelessly to give us the better education we need and for their loving support to us students. The event was held at the Rooftop (6th floor) @ 4:00pm - 6:00pm.
